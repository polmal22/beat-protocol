## How It Works

### 1. Daily Heartbeat
Every verified human generates **10 BEAT/day** (fixed, no growth).

### 2. Fireproof Safety Net
Your first **5,000 BEAT** are protected forever (0% demurrage).

### 3. Progressive Melting
Money above 5,000 melts progressively:
- 5,000-10,000 BEAT: -5%/month
- 10,000-20,000 BEAT: -10%/month  
- 20,000+ BEAT: -15%/month

**Result:** Can't hoard wealth. Must circulate.

### 4. Privacy
- Biometrics stay on device (never uploaded)
- Zero-knowledge proofs for identity
- P2P transfers (no central tracking)

### 5. Local Pricing
- 10 BEAT in Bangkok â‰  10 BEAT in London (purchasing power)
- Communities set their own prices
- No global exchange rate
