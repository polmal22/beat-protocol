# Contributing to Beat Protocol

Thank you for your interest! Beat is community-driven from day one.

## How to Contribute

### 1. Developers

**Current needs:**
- [ ] Android app (Kotlin)
- [ ] iOS app (Swift)  
- [ ] Mint server (Node.js/Go/Rust)
- [ ] Cryptography (ZK proofs implementation)
- [ ] UI/UX design

**Getting started:**
1. Read [WHITEPAPER.md](WHITEPAPER.md)
2. Check [Issues](https://github.com/beat-protocol/beat-protocol/issues)
3. Comment on an issue you want to work on
4. Fork, code, submit PR

**Code standards:**
- Write tests
- Document public APIs
- Follow language conventions
- Security-first mindset

### 2. Economists & Researchers

**Help needed:**
- Economic simulations (agent-based models)
- Game theory analysis
- Social impact studies
- Academic papers

**Process:**
1. Open an issue with your research question
2. Share findings in `/research` folder
3. Discuss with community

### 3. Designers

**Current needs:**
- Logo & visual identity
- App UI/UX mockups
- Marketing materials
- Educational graphics

**Submit:**
- Open PR with designs in `/design` folder
- Share in Discussions for feedback

### 4. Writers & Translators

**Help with:**
- FAQ expansion
- Translation to other languages
- Blog posts
- Educational content

### 5. Community Organizers

**Want to pilot Beat?**

Requirements:
- 50-200 interested people
- Geographic concentration
- 6-month commitment
- Mix of producers/consumers

**Contact:** hello@beat-protocol.org

## Code of Conduct

### Our Values

- **Respect** — Assume good faith, be kind
- **Transparency** — Open discussion, no backdoor deals
- **Inclusion** — Everyone welcome regardless of background
- **Privacy** — Respect user data always
- **Decentralization** — No single points of control

### Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, hate speech
- Spam, trolling, bad faith arguments
- Violating user privacy
- Attempting to centralize control
- Promoting violence or illegal activity

**Violations:** Immediate ban from project spaces.
